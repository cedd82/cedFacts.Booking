FACTS Booking Api

General notes

Facts api is a general purpose vehicle transport booking service provided by CEVA Logistics for interstate vehicle transport. Quotes are provided on a vehicle by vehicle basis.

Vehicles can be moved with three different services:

  • standard - base service to move a vehicle.
  • express - faster transport than standard.
  • premiumEnclosed - ideal for high value vehicles, customers who which to provide extra protection for their vehicle during movement. Vehicles are enclosed in a container preventing damage.

Vehicles can be moved to and from two different location types:

  • customer - a customers home address within Australia.
  • depot - a CEVA logistics depot in an Australian state. Our Depots include:
